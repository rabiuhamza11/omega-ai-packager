# OMEGA DOCMASTER X ULTIMATE
## VERSION 31.0 — VOLUME IX
### ENTERPRISE DATA GOVERNANCE, RECORDS MANAGEMENT & COMPLIANCE
*Classification: Enterprise Information Governance Engineering Specification*

---

## PURPOSE
Establishes policies, services, and controls to manage information throughout its lifecycle. Ensures organizational knowledge remains accurate, trustworthy, searchable, protected, auditable, and managed per governance policies and legal/regulatory requirements.

---

## GOVERNANCE PRINCIPLES
Every information asset shall be: Owned, Classified, Versioned, Searchable, Auditable, Protected, Retained per policy, Recoverable, Disposed through approved processes, Governed by documented responsibilities.

---

## INFORMATION ASSET MODEL
Documents, Records, Reports, Contracts, Policies, Procedures, Drawings, Images, Videos, Audio, Emails, Knowledge articles, Templates, Workflow definitions, Audit logs. Each asset receives a unique identifier and metadata.

---

## METADATA FRAMEWORK
*Identity:* Asset ID, Organization, Workspace, Project.
*Ownership:* Owner, Department, Team.
*Classification:* Information category, Security label, Business function.
*Lifecycle:* Creation date, Review date, Approval date, Archive date, Retention schedule.
*Search:* Tags, Keywords, Language, Related assets.
Organizations may extend the metadata schema while maintaining compatibility.

---

## INFORMATION CLASSIFICATION
Levels: Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted.
Classification influences access controls, handling procedures, and retention policies.

---

## RECORDS MANAGEMENT
Record declaration, Categorization, Retention schedules, Archive management, Legal hold support, Disposition review, Secure disposal, Restoration procedures. All actions recorded in audit logs.

---

## RETENTION MANAGEMENT
Based on: Document type, Department, Business process, Project, Classification, Organizational rules, Legal/regulatory obligations. Platform tracks execution and sends review reminders.

---

## LEGAL HOLD SUPPORT
Place assets on legal hold, Suspend automated disposition, Track legal hold history, Release holds when authorized. All actions fully audited.

---

## VERSION GOVERNANCE
Every revision records: Version number, Author, Timestamp, Change summary, Approval status, Related workflow. Historical versions remain accessible per retention policies.

---

## DATA QUALITY
Evaluate: Completeness, Consistency, Metadata accuracy, Duplicate detection, Relationship integrity, Template compliance. Generate quality reports and recommendations.

---

## PRIVACY CONTROLS
Restrict access, Apply classification labels, Review permissions, Manage retention, Record consent, Support data subject requests per organizational policy and applicable law.

---

## AUDIT GOVERNANCE
Events: Asset creation, Updates, Approval actions, Access requests, Permission changes, Archive actions, Restoration, Policy changes. Audit logs protected from unauthorized modification.

---

## COMPLIANCE MANAGEMENT
Policy documentation, Control mapping, Evidence collection, Audit preparation, Review scheduling, Compliance reporting. Platform assists — does not replace legal/professional advice.

---

## DATA MIGRATION
Validation, Metadata mapping, Integrity checks, Duplicate detection, Error reporting, Rollback planning. Migration results documented for review.

---

## ARCHIVAL SERVICES
Preserve: Metadata, Relationships, Version history, Audit history, Classification, Retention rules. Archived assets remain searchable per permissions.

---

## GOVERNANCE DASHBOARDS
Information inventory, Classification distribution, Retention status, Archive growth, Data quality metrics, Audit activity, Compliance trends, Policy exceptions.

---

## GOVERNANCE METRICS
Metadata completeness, Classification coverage, Retention compliance, Archive utilization, Duplicate reduction, Information quality, Review completion, Audit readiness.

---

## MASTER DIRECTIVE
The Enterprise Data Governance, Records Management & Compliance Platform shall provide a trusted framework for managing organizational information throughout its lifecycle. Help organizations maintain high-quality records, apply consistent governance, preserve institutional knowledge, and support compliance through secure, transparent, and auditable processes.
